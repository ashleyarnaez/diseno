About Us

We are a sanctuary dedicated to the care and conservation of lions. Come and visit our pride!

The Lion Habitat Ranch is a non-profit sanctuary located in Las Vegas, Nevada, dedicated to the care, conservation, and education about lions and other exotic animals. Established as a safe haven for retired animals from the entertainment industry and zoos, our mission is to provide lifelong, loving care in a naturalistic environment.

Visitors can experience up-close encounters with our lions, giraffe painting sessions, and educational talks from our passionate keepers. Every visit supports our efforts in animal welfare, public education, and sustainable conservation practices.

Whether you're a local or a traveler, the Lion Habitat Ranch offers a unique opportunity to connect with wildlife and learn how we can all contribute to protecting these majestic creatures.
